AI Chatbot Developer

View all jobs
Petaling Jaya, Selangor (Hybrid)
Engineering - Software (Information & Communication Technology)
Full time
Add expected salary to your profile for insights
Posted 7d ago
Quick apply

We are seeking individuals with a strong interest and foundational knowledge in AI Chatbot development, who is ready to collaborate closely with our engineers to build a smart, autonomous AI assistant.

The ideal candidate will be passionate about AI, natural language processing (NLP), and working in a cross-functional team environment to deliver customer-centric solutions.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and develop intelligent chatbots using platforms like Dialogflow, Microsoft Bot Framework, Rasa, or similar.

  • Integrate chatbot with knowledge base built by collaborating with Engineers to ensure accurate and context-aware responses.

  • Collaborate with Engineers to gather domain knowledge and technical documentation for chatbot training.

  • Train and optimize chatbot models using NLP techniques, intent recognition, and entity extraction.

  • Implement automation workflows to route complex queries to human agents or internal systems.

  • Monitor and improve chatbot performance using analytics and user feedback.

  • Support integration with CRM, ERP, or e-commerce systems for order and inventory inquiries.

  • Stay updated with latest trends in AI, NLP, and conversational AI for continuous improvement.

What We’re Looking For

Education:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Artificial Intelligence, or related field.

Technical Skills:

  • Experience with AI/ML tools (e.g., Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn).

  • Knowledge of chatbot platforms: Dialogflow, Rasa, Microsoft Bot Framework, or similar.

  • Basic understanding of NLP concepts: Intent recognition, entity extraction, context management.

  • Familiarity with APIs and web integration (REST, JSON, webhooks).

  • Experience with SQL and database management (bonus).

  • Understanding of safety systems or industrial applications (e.g., fire detection, gas monitoring) is a plus.

Soft Skills:

  • Strong communication and problem-solving abilities.

  • Collaborative mindset, especially working with engineers and technical teams.

  • Passion for learning and innovation.

  • Ability to work independently and manage small projects.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Academic projects or internships involving chatbot development or NLP.

  • Experience in domain-specific knowledge base creation.

  • Familiarity with CRM or ERP systems.

  • Exposure to sales and technical support workflows.

What We Offer

  • Opportunity to work in a dynamic, innovative environment.

  • Collaboration with Application Engineers, Sales, and Technical Support teams.

  • Exposure to real-world applications in industrial safety and gas detection.

  • Professional growth and learning opportunities.

  • Competitive salary and performance-based incentives.
